Ties and Tails Gala

November 2, 2024

Get ready for a night like no other! Join us for our Ties and Tails Gala on Saturday, November 2nd, at The Revelry – North End. This black-tie optional, pet-friendly evening celebrates the Humane Society of Charlotte’s lifesaving work and mission. Enjoy dinner, refreshing drinks, bid on incredible prizes at our live and silent auctions, and dance the night away with our live band.

Over 500 animal lovers and their furry friends come together at our Ties and Tails Gala to support HSC. As an independent non-profit organization not affiliated with any national organization, our work depends on the generous support of individuals and donors.

Your support makes a difference. All proceeds from the gala fund essential programs and services that improve the lives of companion animals and the people who care for them.
